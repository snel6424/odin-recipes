Breakfast Tacos

What are Breakfast Tacos?

Breakfast tacos are generally tacos one would eat for breakfast, though they can be consumed at any time of the day. Commonly, these tacos would contain scrambled eggs, which is a common breakfast staple worldwide. Though the origin of the breakfast taco is unknown, they are found regularly near the U.S./Mexico border.

Ingredients

  • Bacon: For the most delicious, savory breakfast crunch.
  • Eggs: Soft, pillowy scrambled eggs are the best.
  • Onion: Adds a pop of fresh flavor with every bite.
  • Avocado: Creamy avocado is always welcome at the breakfast table.
  • Cheese: I'm using shredded cheddar, but feel free to use whatever you have on hand.
  • Herbs: Fresh cilantro is always my go-to.
  • Tortilla: I love to make my cassava flour tortillas, but any kind of tortilla will work!

Instructions

Making breakfast tacos is quick and easy!

  1. Cook the bacon. Bake your strips in the oven for 18-20 minutes, then let them dry on a paper towel.
  2. Saute the green onions. Give the white parts of the green onion a quick saut√© with olive oil.
  3. In goes the eggs. Whisk the eggs and pour it into the pan. Gently stir the eggs as they cook to create the perfect pillowy texture.
  4. Time to assemble! Add a good spoonful or two of scrambled eggs to a tortilla. Top it off with green onion, crumbled bacon, diced avocado, cheese, cilantro, and a dash of salt and pepper.

Pro Tip: Warmed up tortillas are a must for any taco! Gently heat them in a pan or over a gas flame for browned edges.
